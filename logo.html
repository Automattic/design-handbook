Logotype

The WordPress logo was originally created back in 2005 by Jason Santa Maria. The branding associated specifically with WordPress.com has evolved greatly since then, and that's what we'll document here.

The WordPress logo was created in early 2005 by Jason Santa Maria. Emigre's typeface "Mrs Eaves" is used for the logotype, and "Dalliance" (also by Emigre) was used as influence for the "W" mark. The logo was later visually refined by our very own Matt Miklik.
