Color Guide

Because the world is better in technicolor

"Baeta’s" Blue

Lonvingly named after Hugo Baeta, this is the primary WordPress.com blue.

  • WordPress.com Blue
  • #0087be
  • rgb(0,135,190)

Complimentary Blues

These are extra blues for use in situations where the base blues are not a good fit. They work especially well for large blocks of color or other accents.

  • Light Blue
  • #78dcfa
  • rgb(120,220,250)
  • Medium Blue
  • #00aadc
  • rgba(0,170,220)
  • Dark Blue
  • #005082
  • rgb(0,80,130)

Validations and Alerts

These colors are useful for form field validation, alerts, warnings, and pats on the back. Not intended for large blocks of color. Works great with Noticons! Optimized for color blindness.

  • Valid Green
  • #4cb420
  • rgb(76,180,32)
  • Warning Yellow
  • #ffba00
  • rgb(255,186,0)
  • Error Red
  • #dd3d36
  • rgb(221,61,54)
