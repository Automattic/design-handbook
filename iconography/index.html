Iconography

Icons carry a tremendous weight in conveying branding and design language. They give a UI its most recognizable elements of identity when used correctly.

Right now we are in flux as we build Gridicons, an evolved icon set for WordPress.com. These are the icon sets currently available:

  • Noticons (from “notification icons”) is our WordPress.com icon font. It contains every icon “Genericons” has, plus some that are specific to WordPress.com. You can use Genericons for themes and other projects. (Please do not use Noticons outside of their intended 16px font-size or 2x multiples thereof (32px, 64px). Even fonts look fuzzy outside of their pixel grid.)
  • Genericons is an Open Source icon font. Most of it is included in Noticons. Useful for themes and non-WordPress.com stuff.
  • Dashicons is the official icon font of the WordPress admin. It should only be used within /wp-admin/.
