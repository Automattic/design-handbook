Introduction

Welcome to the WordPress.com Design Handbook. These pages are meant to serve as design language guidelines for all that is WordPress.com branded.

In the history of WordPress.com there has never been an underlying design thinking - we're a development-driven company, and it shows. Over the last two years we've attemptend to document and apply some guidelines for consistency across WordPress.com pages and sites to a certain degree of success. This handbook aims to further that engagement on design consistency and brand focus.

All that you'll see written here is meant to serve as a guideline. Ultimately you should use your design experience to make decisions, but keeping in mind that there should be concrete reasons to break these rules.
