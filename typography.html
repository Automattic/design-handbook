Follow a few simple guidelines, and great typography is suddenly within reach.

Open Sans

WordPress.com uses Open Sans as the default text face across all the public areas of WordPress.com. It works great pretty much everywhere, but it's especially great for body copy and UI.

You can choose which variants of Open Sans to include by using Google Fonts.

Merriweather

Merriweather makes a great counterpart to Open Sans. Its best suited for larger font sizes and bolder weights — a great face for titles.

You can choose which variants of Merriweather to include by using Google Fonts.

Aa
  • Merriweather 900
Aa
  • Merriweather 700

Line Height, Font Size, and Modular Scale

Paragraph line-height should be 1.618 (the goldenrod ratio.) For titles and larger type, this feels a little too "spacey," so try to keep those down to 1.258
