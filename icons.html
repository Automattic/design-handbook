Iconography

Beautiful icons that are quick to use, and easy to create.

Noticons

Noticons (note-icons, or not-icons) is our WordPress.com icon font. It contains every icon “Genericons” has, plus a couple of WordPress.com specific ones. You can use Genericons for themes and other projects.

Please do not use Noticons outside of their intended 16px font-size or 2x multiples thereof (32px, 64px). Even fonts look fuzzy outside of their pixel grid.
