Patterns

A collection of blueprints.

Our pattern library is still a work in progress. In our patterns you’ll find various elements that make up layouts on WordPress.com, including form fields, alerts, modal windows, dropdowns, gravatars/blavatars, and more. Patterns are available for Illustrator, Photoshop, and Sketch. A PDF of the patterns is available for those who need it as a visual reference.
